All things speedrunning

It may have been spoiled for you by the Favorites page (or the header menu that's on every page of the site), but I am a speedrunner who does a bit of speedrunning! On these pages, I will tell you the story of how I became a speedrunner, give you some background on my speedgames, and have a showcase of some of my personal speedrunning achievements!

Fair warning: these pages include a LOT of rambling.

For now, the basis of speedrunning for the uninitiated:

Speedrunning, put most simply, is playing a videogame with the goal of completing an objective–usually reaching the end of the game–in the shortest length of time. But I think many of those who don't speedrun won't know that it's about more than just playing games fast. It's equally as much about being part of a community for a game you love, and engaging in friendly competition with yourself and each other.

Each time you see a speedrun, try to imagine how much time the runner has spent practicing, looking for new strategies, sharing their strategies with the community, trying to improve their own personal best time, and helping other runners improve their best times. Then imagine each and every other member in the community that has done the same.

Speedrunning is a collaborative effort. Every speedrun leaderboard with names and times on it has a passionate group of people behind the scenes, all working together to achieve a collective goal.

And THAT, is the art of speedrunning.
